# 3D Reconstruction Research Notes

## Methods compared

| Method | Input | Output | Difficulty |
|--------|-------|--------|------------|
| COLMAP | Multiple photos | 3D point cloud | Medium |
| NeRF | Photos + camera poses | Photorealistic 3D scene | Hard |
| Gaussian Splatting | Photos | Real-time 3D render | Hard |
| Monocular Depth | Single image | Depth map | Easy |

## Why this matters for PreserveMy.World
3D reconstruction allows us to digitally preserve heritage sites that are at risk
of damage or destruction. A visitor can explore a site virtually even after it is gone.
